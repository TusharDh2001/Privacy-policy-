Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Book My Game ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ground booking platform.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and encrypted password
  • Booking Information: Ground preferences, booking dates, time slots, and payment history
  • Profile Data: User role (customer, manager, admin) and account settings

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent on the platform, booking patterns
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address

2.3 Communication Data

  • WhatsApp OTP: Phone numbers used for verification via WhatsApp Business API
  • Support Messages: Communication with customer support

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process ground bookings and payments
  • Send booking confirmations and updates
  • Manage ground availability and schedules

3.2 Communication

  • Send verification codes via WhatsApp
  • Notify users of booking status changes
  • Respond to support inquiries
  • Send important service notifications

3.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Improve features and user experience
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Resolve technical issues

3.4 Legal Compliance

  • Comply with applicable laws
  • Enforce terms of service
  • Protect legal rights and property

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We Share Information With

  • Ground Managers: Name, phone number, and booking details
  • Payment Processors: Payments handled securely by Razorpay
  • WhatsApp Business API: Phone numbers used only for OTP verification

4.2 We Do NOT

  • Sell personal information
  • Share data for marketing without consent
  • Use data beyond this policy

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Storage

  • Secure PostgreSQL databases
  • Redis cache for session management
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment processors

5.2 Security Measures

  • Bcrypt password encryption
  • JWT-based authentication
  • SSL/TLS encryption
  • Role-based access control

5.3 Data Retention

  • Active account data retained while account is active
  • Booking records retained for 3 years
  • Deleted accounts removed within 90 days upon request

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request account deletion
  • Opt out of promotional messages
  • Manage cookie preferences

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

8. Third-Party Services
